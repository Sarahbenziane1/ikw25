Guide for authors

Authors should submit original, well-structured manuscripts following IKW’s formatting guidelines. All submissions undergo rigorous peer review and must be in Word or LaTeX format.

Policies and guidelines

IKW adheres to strict ethical publishing standards, ensuring originality, peer-review integrity, and transparency. Authors, reviewers, and editors must follow the journal’s guidelines on plagiarism, conflicts of interest, and research ethics.

Open access options

IKW is an open-access journal, ensuring free and immediate access to all published articles. Authors retain copyright under a Creative Commons license, promoting global knowledge dissemination.
