IKW – International Journal of Engineering Innovations is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research across all engineering disciplines. Covering fields such as electrical, mechanical, civil, computer, biomedical, and industrial engineering, IKW serves as a global platform for advancing scientific knowledge and technological innovation. The journal welcomes original research articles, review papers, and technical reports, ensuring a rigorous peer-review process. IKW aims to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and contribute to the latest advancements in engineering science and technology.

IKW25 - Aims

IKW aims to be a premier platform for publishing cutting-edge research across all engineering disciplines. The journal is committed to:
- Promoting scientific advancements and technological innovations in engineering.
- Encouraging interdisciplinary collaboration among researchers and industry professionals.
- Ensuring a rigorous peer-review process to maintain high research integrity.
- Providing open access to high-quality research for a global audience.
- Supporting the dissemination of knowledge that addresses real-world engineering challenges.

Scope

IKW publishes original research articles, review papers, and technical reports across all fields of engineering, including but not limited to:
- Electrical & Electronics Engineering (Embedded systems, AI, IoT, control systems, power systems)
- Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering (Robotics, fluid dynamics, materials science, thermal systems)
- Civil & Structural Engineering (Smart cities, sustainable construction, geotechnical engineering)
- Computer & Software Engineering (Cybersecurity, AI/ML, cloud computing, distributed systems)
- Biomedical & Bioengineering (Medical imaging, wearable devices, biomechanics, biomaterials)
- Industrial & Manufacturing Engineering (Automation, logistics, supply chain, Industry 4.0)
- Chemical & Environmental Engineering (Sustainable energy, nanotechnology, process engineering)
IKW encourages interdisciplinary research and welcomes contributions that push the boundaries of engineering science and technology.

Guide for authors

Authors should submit original, well-structured manuscripts following IKW’s formatting guidelines. All submissions undergo rigorous peer review and must be in Word or LaTeX format.

Policies and guidelines

IKW adheres to strict ethical publishing standards, ensuring originality, peer-review integrity, and transparency. Authors, reviewers, and editors must follow the journal’s guidelines on plagiarism, conflicts of interest, and research ethics.

Open access options

IKW is an open-access journal, ensuring free and immediate access to all published articles. Authors retain copyright under a Creative Commons license, promoting global knowledge dissemination.
